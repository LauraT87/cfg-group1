Swing Dancing

Swing dance is a group of social dances that developed with the swing style of jazz music in the 1920s–1940s, with the origins of each dance predating the popular "swing era".

What is Swing Dancing?

Swing dancing is a popular social dance that is often characterized by lifts, spins and flips. It's generally upbeat, lively and a lot of fun to watch—and to dance! Like most dance styles, there are variations of the dance. It includes some basic steps and then dancers have creative freedom to make it their own.

Getting Started

The great thing about the Swing is that it has basic steps that can be used for other dances. So, once you learn the Swing, you can adapt it and learn other dances. There are just a few basic steps, but the key is shifting your weight to different feet at different times.

Supplies

You don't need to wear anything special. Just wear something you're comfortable moving around in, and be aware that swing dancing can get pretty hot.
